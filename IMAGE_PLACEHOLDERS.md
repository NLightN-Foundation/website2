# Image Placeholders Guide

This document lists all image placeholders that need to be added to the website.

## Required Images

### Logo
- **Path**: `images/logo.png`
- **Location**: Hero section
- **Size**: Recommended max-height: 80px
- **Description**: NLightN Foundation logo

### Announcement Banner
- **Path**: `images/announcement.jpg`
- **Location**: Announcement banner section (below hero)
- **Size**: Recommended: 120x120px
- **Description**: Image for the announcement banner (similar to CAST's AI survey image)

### Help Section Images
- **Path**: `images/access.jpg`
- **Location**: "Access" help card
- **Size**: Recommended: Full width, height: 200px
- **Description**: Image representing access to educational resources

- **Path**: `images/innovation.jpg`
- **Location**: "Innovation" help card
- **Size**: Recommended: Full width, height: 200px
- **Description**: Image representing innovation in education

- **Path**: `images/impact.jpg`
- **Location**: "Impact" help card
- **Size**: Recommended: Full width, height: 200px
- **Description**: Image representing impact in education

### Partner Logos
- **Path**: `images/partners/nlightn-logo.png`
- **Description**: NLightN Foundation logo (for partners section)

- **Path**: `images/partners/csr-partner1.png`
- **Description**: CSR Partner 1 logo

- **Path**: `images/partners/csr-partner2.png`
- **Description**: CSR Partner 2 logo

## Notes
- All images have fallback placeholders that will display if images are not found
- Images should be optimized for web (compressed, appropriate formats)
- Recommended formats: PNG for logos, JPG for photos
