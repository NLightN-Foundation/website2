NLightN

We help students and educational institutions make the fullest use of AI technology to enhance learning outcomes and educational access. With Education as our key vertical, we work to remove barriers, create opportunities, and provide free access to quality educational resources.

All of our programmes and initiatives are free of charge, supported by our foundation and CSR partnerships.
